Who We Are

Chengula Poultry Farm is dedicated to raising the highest quality indigenous chickens using traditional and sustainable methods. We believe in free-range farming, allowing our birds to grow in a stress-free, natural environment.

Our commitment to quality ensures that every egg and piece of meat that leaves our farm is nutritious, flavorful, and safe for your family.
